TRAVEL

Island Information

If you are unfamiliar with Mackinac Island, there are a few key things you should know. Mackinac Island allows no motorized transportation. That means that you will need to take a ferry over to the island from either Mackinaw City or St Ignace. If you are driving North towards the Mackinac Bridge, we recommend taking the ferry from Mackinaw City to save you the cost of the bridge fare.

To the Island

There are two main ferry companies that provide transportation to the island. Sheplers and Starline. We recommend Sheplers for a bit more of a comfortable experience. But nobody is stopping you if you want to ride aboard Starline and see the "rooster tail" from their boat.

On the Island

Once you arrive on the island, you have a few options to get to your hotel or B&B. There will be Porters at the end of the dock who are available, as part of a gratutity based system, to take your luggage to your hotel or B&B for you. You can also call a horse taxi to take you and your luggage up to your hotel or B&B (charging about $12 per person). Or you are free to enjoy the scenery and take in all the sights and smells that come along with the Island experience as you walk to your destination!

Once you are all settled in, there are bike rental shops scattered throughout town that offer hourly and overnight rentals if you are looking for a bit more speed as you explore the Island.

One more important thing to note if you are staying at Mission Point: They offer a hotel shuttle, for $8 per person, that swings by the Starline and Sheplers dock every hour and will take you directly to the hotel.

FAQs

What should I wear? Is there a dress code?

The dress code for our wedding is semi-formal/cocktail attire.

What will the weather be like?

Mackinac Island tends to linger in the mid to high 70s in the middle of July. Expect plenty of wind. I would pack a jacket and pants for the cooler weather at night.

What happens after the ceremony?

The wedding party and bride and groom will be taking pictures for an hour. Guests can head to the promenade deck for a drink at the cash bar until the reception starts

Did I read that right, is it going to be a cash bar? Will there be an open bar?

The cocktail hour will be a cash bar. However, There will be an open bar from 5pm until 8pm, so be sure to get your drinks in while you can!

Are kids welcome?

While we love your little ones, our wedding is going to be an adults-only event so that everyone can relax and enjoy the evening. We appreciate you making arrangements ahead of time and leaving the kids at home so you can celebrate with us.

Do I have to spend the night on the Island?

Of course not! The last boat to leave the island will be at 10:00pm (Occassionaly Starline will offer a midnight boat, but be sure to confirm this before relying on it), so you are free to take that boat over and save a chunk of change on a hotel in St Igance or Mackinaw City.
